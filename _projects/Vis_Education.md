An important part of our research is the development of educational tools and frameworks that can help students and professionals learn how to create effective visualizations. We have developed several frameworks, such as the [Five-Design Sheets (FDS)](https://ritsos.github.io/projects/FDS/), which provides a structured approach to visualization design, and the [Explanatory Visualization Framework (EVF)](https://ritsos.github.io/projects/EVF/), which focuses on teaching creative computing through visualization. Our latest work, the [Critical Design Strategy (CDS)](https://ritsos.github.io/projects/CDS/), introduces a structured heuristic evaluation method designed to facilitate the examination of visualization designs through reflection and critical thought.
