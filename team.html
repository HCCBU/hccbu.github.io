Academics

Panagiotis (Panos) D. Ritsos

I am a Senior Lecturer in Visualization, at the School of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering, Bangor University, UK, and lead the XRVA) Lab. My research interests lie in the domain of human-computer interaction, and include mixed and virtual reality, information visualization and visual analytics. I am particularly interested in potential synergies between these fields within different flavours of human-data interaction, such as beyond-the-desktop visualization, and ubiquitous, situated and immersive analytics. You can find more about me at my personal page.
