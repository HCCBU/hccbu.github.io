Our paper [Preparedness for visualization in the next pandemic]({{site.baseurl}}/publicationsYear#McGee-et-al-CGA-2025) has been published in [IEEE Computer Graphics and Applications](https://www.computer.org/csdl/magazine/cg). This article discusses considerations on how visualization can be best positioned to help response to future pandemics. The work is a product of the Dagstuhl Seminar 23482 on "Visualization for Pandemic Preparedness" held in January 2024. You can find more info on the seminar [here](https://www.dagstuhl.de/en/program/calendar/semhp/?semnr=23482).
