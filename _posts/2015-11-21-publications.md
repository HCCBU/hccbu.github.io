A journal paper, in which we have contributed the design of a Handheld Augmented Reality application for cultural heritage was published in ACM Journal on Computing and Cultural Heritage (JOCCH). The paper provides an overview of the _Heritage Together_ project, lead by Bangor University.

More information can be obtained from the projects website, [HeritageTogether.org](http://heritagetogether.org/?lang=en) or the project's twitter page [@heritagetogether](https://twitter.com/heritage2gether).
