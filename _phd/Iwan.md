Iwan is currently a PhD student at the [School of Computer Science and Engineering](http://www.cs.bangor.ac.uk/), [Bangor University, UK](http://www.bangor.ac.uk/) specialising in data visualisation. He has a background in psychology and cognitive neuroscience where he conducted research in the fields of visual perception, functional magnetic resonance imaging (fMRI), and visual attention. His current field of research pertains to explanatory visualisation and how AI can be used to enhance and extend its benefits and effects. His work is supported by the [AIMLAC](https://cdt-aimlac.org/) programme, funded by UKRI. [More info](https://www.bangor.ac.uk/research-students/scse/iwan-pritchard-023774/en).
