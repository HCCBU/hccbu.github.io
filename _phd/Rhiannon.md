Rhiannon is a PhD candidate in the [School of Computer Science and Engineering](http://www.cs.bangor.ac.uk/), [Bangor University, UK](http://www.bangor.ac.uk/), researching storytelling through data visualisation, with a particular focus on healthcare visualisation. Her work is supported by the [AIMLAC](https://cdt-aimlac.org/) programme, funded by UKRI. [More info](https://www.bangor.ac.uk/research-students/scse/rhiannon-owen-446348/en).
