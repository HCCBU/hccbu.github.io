Welcome to the webpage of the Human-Centred Computing research group, at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Bangor University.

Our research interests and activities lie in the domain of human-computer interaction, and include mixed and virtual reality, information visualization and visual analytics. We are particularly interested in potential synergies between these fields within different flavours of human-data interaction, such as beyond-the-desktop visualization, and ubiquitous, situated and immersive analytics.

Our research contributes mainly to our School's research theme of Informatics, with frequent collaborations with other research groups active in the themes of Photonics and Comminications, and Energy and Biosensing.

We also frequently collaborate with other Schools in BU, as well as severa international collaborators.

In this website you can find news about our activities, people associated with the group, our publications and dissemination activities, and contact information.
